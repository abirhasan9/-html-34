JAPAN

The national flag of Japan, officially known as the Nisshōki (sun-sun flag) but commonly called the Hinomaru (circle of the sun), features a deep red crimson circle representing the sun centered on a white rectangular field. Adopted in 1999, it symbolizes the "Land of the Rising Sun," with white representing peace and honesty, and red representing bravery.
